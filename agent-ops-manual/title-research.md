---

## Title Analysis Framework

### SEO Factors
1. **Keyword targeting** - What people search for
2. **Click-through rate (CTR)** - What makes people click
3. **Credibility signals** - What builds trust
4. **Specificity** - Concrete vs vague promises
5. **Benefit clarity** - What's in it for them

### Copywriting Hooks
1. **Curiosity gap** - Creates desire to know more
2. **Problem-solution** - Addresses pain points
3. **Transformation promise** - Before → After
4. **Specificity** - Numbers, timeframes, results
5. **Authority** - Expert positioning

---
