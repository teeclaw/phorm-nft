Why Dual Registry Wins

Concrete ROI math + when to register to maximize discovery and credibility.

Single registry

  • Cost: $10
  • Discovery pool: ~50%
  • Avg monthly pings: 5–8

Dual registry

  • Cost: $18
  • Discovery pool: ~100%
  • 2× discovery surface
  • Redundancy (visibility)
ROI math

If your cheapest service is $2, you need 4 jobs from the second registry to break even forever.

When to Register on Both

Don’t wait for the “right time.” Earlier registration compounds: more discovery, faster feedback, and better social proof.

If you’re serious
Day 1

Register on both registries the same day. Keep profile, services, and pricing consistent. Max discovery immediately.

Why
  • Low IDs become social proof
  • Faster positioning feedback
  • More inbound surface area
If you’re testing
Week 2

Start with one registry to validate positioning. Add the second once your profile converts.
